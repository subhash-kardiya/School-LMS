# School-Specific Learning Management System (LMS)
## Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

## 1. Introduction (School Context)
### 1.1 Purpose
This document defines the school-specific software requirements for a Learning Management System (LMS) intended for primary, secondary, and higher secondary schools. It addresses academic delivery, student monitoring, parent communication, and administrative control.

### 1.2 Scope
The School LMS will support digital classrooms, homework, examinations, attendance, grading, communication with parents, and academic reporting. The system will be accessible to school management, teachers, students, and parents.

## 2. User Roles (School-Specific)
Role Description
Super Admin System-level control
School Admin School operations and configuration
Teacher Academic delivery and evaluation
Student Learning and assessments
Parent Student monitoring and communication

## 3. School-Specific Functional Requirements
### 3.1 Academic Structure Management
FR-S1: System shall support academic year management.
FR-S2: System shall manage classes, sections, and subjects.
FR-S3: System shall map teachers to subjects and classes.

### 3.2 Student Management
FR-S4: Admin shall create and manage student profiles.
FR-S5: Student records shall include roll number, class, and section.
FR-S6: Promotion of students to the next academic year shall be supported.

### 3.3 Teacher Management
FR-S7: Admin shall manage teacher profiles.
FR-S8: Teachers shall access assigned classes and subjects.

### 3.4 Timetable Management
FR-S9: Admin shall create class-wise timetables.
FR-S10: Teachers and students shall view timetables.

### 3.5 Attendance Management
FR-S11: Teachers shall mark daily attendance.
FR-S12: Parents shall view student attendance.
FR-S13: Attendance reports shall be downloadable.

### 3.6 Homework and Assignments
FR-S14: Teachers shall assign homework.
FR-S15: Students shall submit homework online.
FR-S16: Teachers shall review and provide feedback.

### 3.7 Examination and Results
FR-S19: Teachers shall create exams and tests.
FR-S20: System shall support marks and grades.
FR-S21: Report cards shall be generated.
FR-S22: Parents shall view exam results.

### 3.9 Communication
FR-S23: School shall send notices and announcements.
FR-S24: Teachers shall communicate with parents.

### 3.10 Certificates and Documents
FR-S25: System shall generate bonafide and leaving certificates.

## 6. Acceptance Criteria (School LMS)
All academic workflows function as per the school calendar.
Attendance, exams, and reports are accurate.
Parent access is restricted to assigned student only.
System is responsive and secure.
